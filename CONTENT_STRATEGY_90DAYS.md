## Part 4: Guide & Lead Magnet Strategy

### Why Lead Magnets Matter for Your Business
As a solo developer with limited marketing budget, lead magnets are your most cost-effective way to:
1. Build email list for future nurturing
2. Pre-qualify leads (who downloads = serious about their project)
3. Establish authority
4. Generate repeat traffic

**Size:** 2-3 pages, printable

**Content:**

- Must-have features checklist
- Design considerations
- Budget estimation worksheet
- Technical requirements
- Timeline planner
- Red flags to watch for
- Next steps after completion

**CTA Strategy:** "Get your free Website Planning Checklist"
