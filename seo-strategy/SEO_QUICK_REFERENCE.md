## FAQ: Quick Answers

**Q: How long until we see results?**
A: 2-4 weeks for first positions, 2-3 months for significant traffic, 6+ months for full strategy impact.

**Q: How many keywords should we target?**
A: Start with top 20 keywords, add 10-15 more each month as content accumulates.

**Q: Should we create video content?**
A: Yes, but after text content. Repurpose blog posts into YouTube videos.

**Q: What about guest posting?**
A: Yes, but only after Month 2. Focus on building your own authority first.

**Q: How often should we publish?**
A: 1-2 articles per week for first 12 weeks, then maintain 2-4 per month.

**Q: Should we target English keywords?**
A: Yes, after Hebrew keywords are secure. Use same content, optimize for English audience.
