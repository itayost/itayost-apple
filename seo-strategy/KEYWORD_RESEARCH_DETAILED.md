## Part 1: Keyword Research Methodology

### Search Intent Categories

**1. Commercial Intent (Hiring Intent) - HIGHEST PRIORITY**
- User is actively looking to hire someone/buy service
- Keywords: "בניית אתרים", "פיתוח אפליקציה", "מערכת CRM"
- CTR potential: 8-15% (when ranking)
- Conversion rate: 5-15%

**2. Transactional Intent (Purchase/Signup)**
- User wants to complete a transaction or get quote
- Keywords: "דף נחיתה בניית", "חנות אונליין בנייה"
- CTR potential: 5-8%
- Conversion rate: 3-8%

**3. Informational Intent (Learning)**
- User wants to learn about something
- Keywords: "מה זה CRM?", "כיצד לפתוח חנות אונליין"
- CTR potential: 3-5%
- Conversion rate: 2-5% (through nurture)

**4. Navigational Intent (Looking for you)**
- User looking for your site or specific page
- Keywords: "ITAYOST", "מערכות ואוטומציות"
- CTR potential: 50%+
- Conversion rate: 10-20%

## Part 3: Search Intent Deep Dive

### User Journey by Keyword

#### Intent Stage 1: Awareness/Problem Recognition
**Keywords:** "מה זה CRM?", "איך להתחרות בתחרותים?"
**Content Type:** Educational blog posts, explanatory videos
**Goal:** Educate about problem + your solution
**Conversion:** 1-2% (very early stage)

#### Intent Stage 2: Consideration (Comparing Solutions)
**Keywords:** "Next.js vs WordPress", "CRM vs Excel", "Make vs Zapier"
**Content Type:** Comparison posts, feature tables, pros/cons
**Goal:** Position your service favorably
**Conversion:** 5-8% (stronger intent)

#### Intent Stage 3: Decision (Ready to Hire)
**Keywords:** "בניית אתרים", "מערכת CRM בעברית", "אוטומציה לעסקים"
**Content Type:** Service pages, pricing, case studies
**Goal:** Close the sale
**Conversion:** 10-20% (highest intent)

#### Intent Stage 4: Retention (Post-purchase)
**Keywords:** "כיצד להשתמש ב-CRM", "How to optimize landing page"
**Content Type:** Tutorials, documentation, FAQs
**Goal:** Onboarding + upsell
**Conversion:** 10-30% (internal sales)

## Part 9: Keyword Opportunity Score Formula

### Calculation:
```
Opportunity Score = (Search Volume × Relevance × Conversion Intent) / Difficulty
```

**Example Calculations:**

1. **"בניית אתר לעסק" (Build website for business)**
   - Volume: 800 / Competition: 35
   - Relevance: 100% / Difficulty: 30
   - Score = (800 × 1.0 × 0.95) / 30 = **25.3** (HIGH PRIORITY)

2. **"כמה עולה לפתח אתר" (How much does website cost)**
   - Volume: 580 / Competition: 20
   - Relevance: 90% / Difficulty: 15
   - Score = (580 × 0.9 × 0.98) / 15 = **34.1** (HIGHEST PRIORITY)

3. **"עיצוב ממשק משתמש" (UI/UX Design)**
   - Volume: 320 / Competition: 40
   - Relevance: 85% / Difficulty: 45
   - Score = (320 × 0.85 × 0.92) / 45 = **5.8** (LOWER PRIORITY)
