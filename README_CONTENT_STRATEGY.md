## The Most Important Things

### Don't Optimize Prematurely
Start with content quality and consistency. SEO improvements come later.

### Publish Consistently
10 imperfect articles > 2 perfect articles. Start the cadence.

### Internal Link Everything
Use strategic internal linking to pass authority between pages.

### Measure What Matters
Traffic, keywords, leads. Everything else is noise.

### Adjust Based on Data
After Month 1, see what's working and double down on it.

### Don't Give Up
SEO takes 3-6 months to show real results. Stick to the plan.
